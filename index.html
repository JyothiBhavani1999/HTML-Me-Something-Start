My Recent visit to Andaman and Nicobar Islands 💗

Introduction

Craving a summer escape, my friends and I impulsively booked a flight to the Andaman Islands. Little did we know, paradise awaited!

Beauty of Andaman

The Andamans are like a postcard come to life. Picture-perfect beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush jungles made every moment feel like a dream. Each island offers a unique blend of natural beauty that captivates every visitor.

Food

Our taste buds went on vacation too! We feasted on the freshest seafood, spicy curries, and sweet coconut treats. Trust me, your mouth will thank you. One of the highlights of the trip was the local cuisine.

  • Grilled Fish
  • Seafood Biryani
  • Freshly Squeezed Coconut Water
  • and more Thaalis

Activities and Adventures

SNORKELED. SCUBA DIVED. KAYAKED!

Everyday was a new experience. From diving into the deep blue sea to exploring the vibrant marine life, every day was packed with adventure.

  • Snorkeling at Elephant Beach
  • Scuba Diving at Havelock Islandt
  • Kayaking at Neil Islands
